About Us

Best Customer Experience

We Are --ALM Autos is a company located in Albania which is focused in car rental business providing reliable, cheap alternatives of transportation which enables you to go anywhere without waiting for buses, taxi at any time. Visiting Albania for tourism or work travel can be easier and you can enjoy this travel better with our car rental as a way of transport. Our business is known for the importance we attach to customer satisfaction, ethical business behaviour and respect towards each other. We work by these principles and adhere to them at all times

Company Mission --Our mission is providing reliable, economical cars and friendly services that makes customers confident that we are the best car rental choice. Our vision is a future where car-renting outnumber car owners as more profitable and reliable way of transport. We value our clients as business partners. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality of service to them and will treat their goals and objectives as our own. We will strive to earn our customers loyalty by working to deliver more than promised, being honest and fair and to provide exceptional personalized service that creates a pleasing business experience
